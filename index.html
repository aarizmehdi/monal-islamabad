Our Story

Where the Mountains Meet the Plate

Since 1998, Monal has defined Pakistani dining — from a single hillside restaurant to a beloved institution across six cities.

A Legacy of Flavour

What began as a small restaurant on the Margalla Hills has grown into Pakistan's most celebrated dining group — without ever compromising the quality that made it famous.

Our chefs masterfully blend traditional recipes with contemporary techniques, sourcing ingredients from local markets across the country. Every dish carries the memory of generations.
