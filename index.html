Our Story

Islamabad's Most Iconic Dining Destination

Margalla Hills, Islamabad

Where the Mountains Meet the Plate

Nestled at 1,500 meters above sea level on the scenic Pir Sohawa Road, Monal has been a beloved institution since 1998. What began as a small hillside restaurant has grown into Islamabad's most celebrated dining experience, drawing visitors from across Pakistan and around the world.

Our chefs masterfully blend traditional Pakistani recipes with contemporary techniques, sourcing ingredients from local markets and our own herb gardens. Every dish tells a story — of the land, the culture, and the hands that prepared it.
